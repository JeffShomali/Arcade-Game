Welcome to Arcade Game.

In this game you have a Player and Enemies (Bugs). The goal of the player is to reach the water, without colliding into any one of the enemies. The player can move left, right, up and down. The enemies move in varying speeds on the paved block portion of the scene. Once a the player collides with an enemy, the game is reset and the player moves back to the start square. Once the player reaches the water the game is won. Here is the sample of finished project on Youtube.

How to Play:

Move the player by pressing the arrows Up, Down, Left or Right. Avoid the bugs, collect the gems.
